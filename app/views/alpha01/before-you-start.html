Before you start

To apply for Employment and Support Allowance:

  • you must have a health condition of injury
  • you must not be getting Jobseeker's Allowance or Universal Credit
  • you must not be involved in industrial action (eg a strike, walk-out or lock-out)
  • you must not be working over 16 hours per week (paid and voluntary work)
